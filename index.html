About Me

Where Creativity
Meets Strategy

My name is Charlotte Elizabeth Vogel. I hold a dual Bachelor's degree in International Business from Hochschule Harz (Germany) and in Business Management & Marketing from Burgundy School of Business (France), both completed in October 2024.

My experience spans three countries and multiple industries: from managing business operations at a hospitality venture in Panama, to developing digital marketing content for a software company in Germany, to leading the social media marketing of an entire academic programme as a student project manager.

I'm drawn to roles where strategy meets creativity. I value building brand presence, crafting targeted campaigns, and navigating multicultural outreach. I speak conversational French and functional German, and thrive in international, cross-functional teams.
